Our Work

Every year the nation requires about 4 Crore units of blood, out of which only a meager 40 Lakh units of blood are available. There are multiple blood banks around the world, however none of them offer the capability for a direct contact between the donor and recipient. In majority of the cases the blood ends up not reaching the patients due the formalities that are meant to be cleared. "VCE Blood Bank" proposes to bring voluntary blood donors and those in need of blood on to a common platform.
